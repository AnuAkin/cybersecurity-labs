# Intro to Cyber Security – TryHackMe

## Overview
Completed introductory cybersecurity training focused on foundational security concepts, common cyber threats, and the role of cybersecurity in protecting systems and data.

## Topics Covered
- CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
- Types of cyber attacks and threat actors
- Phishing, malware, and social engineering
- Basic security principles and best practices
- Introduction to defensive and offensive security concepts

## Skills Developed
- Understanding core cybersecurity terminology
- Identifying common attack methods
- Recognizing the importance of risk management and security awareness
- Building foundational knowledge for future cybersecurity labs and certifications

## Takeaways
This module helped build a strong foundation in cybersecurity concepts and provided a better understanding of how cyber threats impact individuals and organizations.
